Executive Board

Meet the passionate leaders who drive our mission and keep the music playing for those who serve.

Leadership Team

Our executive board is made up of community members, veterans, musicians, and advocates who share a passion for service and the arts.

🎸

President

Executive Officer

Leads the overall vision and strategic direction of Battle of the Bands, INC, ensuring the organization fulfills its mission to veterans and first responders throughout the region.

🎺

Vice President

Operations

Oversees day-to-day operations, event logistics, and organizational partnerships – including our collaboration with RedWater Entertainment.

🎹

Secretary

Administration

Manages organizational records, meeting minutes, and official correspondence, ensuring smooth governance and legal compliance for the nonprofit.

🥁

Treasurer

Finance

Responsible for financial oversight, fund management, and ensuring that every dollar raised goes toward meaningful support for veterans and first responders in need.

🎙️

Events Director

Event Management

Leads the planning and execution of all major events including the Vet Jam Summer Fest and the Karaoke Invitational, coordinating with venues, artists, and sponsors.

📣

Community Outreach Director

Outreach & Partnerships

Builds and nurtures relationships with veterans' organizations, first responder groups, local businesses, and community stakeholders who share our commitment to service.
