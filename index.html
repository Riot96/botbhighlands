Who We Are

The Battle of the Bands, Incorporated is a Highlands County, FL 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting veterans and first responders through the power of music and community events.

Through our flagship events – the Vet Jam concert series and our renowned Karaoke Invitational – we unite musicians, fans, and local businesses to raise critical funds for those who have sacrificed in service of our nation and communities.

We work hand-in-hand with our partner RedWater Entertainment to produce world-class experiences that make giving back feel like a celebration.
